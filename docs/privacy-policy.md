# Privacy Policy

**App name:** Aura  
**Last updated:** May 6, 2026

---

## Overview

Aura is a personal AI assistant app. This policy explains what data Aura accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected.

---

## Google Calendar Access

Aura requests access to your Google Calendar to:

- Read your calendar events to provide scheduling context to the AI assistant
- Create, update, or delete events on your behalf when you explicitly ask the assistant to do so

Aura does **not**:
- Share your calendar data with third parties
- Use your calendar data for advertising or profiling
- Store calendar data beyond what is needed to respond to your request

---

## Data We Collect

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Google account identifier | To associate your session with your calendar |
| Calendar events (read) | To give the assistant scheduling context |
| Messages you send to the assistant | To generate responses |

---

## Data Storage

- Calendar data is fetched live per request and is not persisted to any database.
- Chat messages may be stored to support conversation history features within the app.
- No data is sold or shared with third parties.

---

## Third-Party Services

Aura uses the following services:

- **Google Calendar API** — to read and write calendar events
- **Anthropic / Google Gemini** — to power AI responses
- **Firebase** — for authentication and data storage
- **LiveKit** — for voice features

Each service is governed by its own privacy policy.

---

## Data Deletion

You can revoke Aura's access to your Google account at any time by visiting [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions) and removing Aura from the list of connected apps.

To request deletion of any stored app data, contact us at the email below.
